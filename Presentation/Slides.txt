DCA - Dollar Cost Averaging
The vanilla version:
- Invest a fixed amount of money at regular intervals (e.g., monthly) regardless of the stock price.
- Benefits: Reduces the impact of volatility, lowers the average cost per share over time.
- Example: Investing $500 every month in NVDA or TSLA.
